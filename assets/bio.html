Autobiography

Born in northern Kentucky, I have been a native to the area my entire life. Both of my parents have been an important part of my identity; from birth to present. Much of my childhood interests involved technology or farm living. I built RC drones, grew a garden, raised animals, and tinkered with cars. As an adult most of these things I still do. In high school I took high level course such as honors chemistry, physics, and personal finance. I graduated with a 3.75 GPA and placed very high among my class in terms of grades. I love hearing new ideas in business and technology and hope to join or create a startup on day.
